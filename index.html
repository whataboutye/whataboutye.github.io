Belfast's neighbourhoods
Each part of the city has its own character. Here's where to go for what.
Arts & nightlife
Cathedral Quarter
Belfast's creative hub — galleries, independent bars, live music and the best restaurants in the city.
Heritage & culture
Titanic Quarter
Where the Titanic was built. Now home to the world-class museum, film studios and waterfront dining.
Village vibes
South Belfast
Botanic Avenue, the Ormeau Road and beautiful Botanic Gardens — Belfast's most laid-back quarter.
Shopping & history
City Centre
Victorian architecture, St George's Market, the Crown Bar and all the big-name shopping.
Community & culture
East Belfast
Van Morrison's birthplace and home to some of Belfast's most colourful and historic murals.
Seaside escapes
North Down coast
Bangor, Holywood and Donaghadee — beautiful coastal towns just 20 minutes from the city centre.
